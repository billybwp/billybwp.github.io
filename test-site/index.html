If you doubt the weight, don't lift it.

Don't lift too much weight on the gym. It will lead into an injury, i just learned my lesson yesterday when i broke my right forearm bone, it may be just a minor fracture, but the accident make me cannot go to the gym for at least 3 weeks.

And if you happen to have accident like me at the gym, it will make your life harder:

  • You cannot eat easily.
  • Filling glass of water will be hard
  • Taking a shower and washing your back will become so fucking hard.

what's more annoying is that you have to endure this pain for about 1 week before you can easily do your daily task again!

So the point is, gym accident sucks even though the accident is minor, it is still very sucks. Do not lift too much weight. Only lift when you are confident with the weight.
